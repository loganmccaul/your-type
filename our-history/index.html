About

Your-Type is a Small Family-Owned Business
‍
Your-Type is a small family-owned business currently operated out of Winona, MN. 

With over 30 years of experience in the signage industry, founder and CEO Todd Hoffman has done everything from hand-painted signs to specialized exterior and interior installs for just about any and everyone that has something they need to say with a sign. 

From left: Bill H., Todd Hoffman, Steve J., and Bob M.
 
Passion for Fonts & Craftmaship
‍
It is Todd's passion for fonts and his personal craftsmanship that brought Your-Type to life.

It's no surprise that Todd's love of fonts actually began as a young boy using pen and ink to make different fonts, and that passion grew into the signage business that has served him and his family well for the past 30 years. Throughout his career, he has enjoyed finding ways to engage his audience in a way that leaves them feeling proud to have been a part of a community, college or business. And Your-Type is an extension of that passion and hands-on approach that Todd and his family have in their work. 

Currently, Your-Type includes Todd's wife and college graduate daughter on the team.
 
 
Your Business Really Matters
‍
You can trust that your business matters to Todd and his family. They take great care to ensure that your letters are delivered on time, and will leave an impression on your event. 

Todd and his family look forward to seeing how you use giant, 3-D letters to create a unique experience for your next event. If you have any questions or need anything, please do reach out and let us know. We are happy to help
